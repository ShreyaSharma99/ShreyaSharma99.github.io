About

Hi! I am Shreya Sharma, a graduate student pursuing my Master's in Robotics (fully funded program) at Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh. I am working at Advanced Agents Robotics Technology (AART) Lab advised by Prof. Katia Sycara. I will be graduating in May 2024.

I completed my dual degree (B.Tech + M.Tech) in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi in 2022. My thesis at IIT Delhi was in Neuro-symbolic AI and NLP and was co-advised by Prof. Mausam and Prof. Rohan Paul.

I worked as a research fellow for one semester under Prof. Mrinmaya Sachan at ETH, Zurich, in Reinforcement learning and NLP. I have interned with Prof. Kalyanmoy Deb in Genetic Algorithms at Michigan State University.

My academic journey has nurtured my interests in Reinforcement Learning, Vision-Language models, and, in general, all practical applications that ML brings to the table. I have a strong background in math fundamentals, including probability theory and statistics.
